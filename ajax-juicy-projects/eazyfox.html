EazyFox
A marketplace for business consultants and businesses.
Project Description
Eazyfox is a marketplace for businesses in need of management and business consultants. People can post their requriement and their budget, based on which freelance consultants can bid and get projects to work on. The entire product was built by our team using Laravel framework in the backend and Angular in the frontend.
